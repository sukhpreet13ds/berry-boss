Your Safety is Our Number One Priority

Food safety is our number one priority at BBI Produce. It's our job to make sure all produce that leaves our farms is safe for your family. Food safety is an integral part of our operation. Food safety impacts the growing, harvest, transport, packing, cooling, storage, and shipping of our produce. From our field practices to our tracking of every box of fruit, we can ensure our products remain safe all the way to your fridge.

  • All farms are GFS certified by Primus Labs.
  • Packing shed GFS certified by Primus Labs.
  • Ranch audit and Harvest Crew audit.
  • PTI (Produce Traceability Initiative) compliant.
  • All flats/cartons/bins labeled for traceability.
  • GTIN on all clamshells.
